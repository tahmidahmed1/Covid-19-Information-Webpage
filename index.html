Editors note



The coronavirus has had a tremendous effect on hunmanity. In order to get through these tough times, people must come together and unite in any sort of way to help battle this deadly virus. Our mission with this website is to give people easy access to Corona Virus resources and Statistics that is reliable and accurrate. We even added some intriguing features like a map that shows COVID-19 stats being distributed in regions based on its own subregions. We hope our website can give an easy access for visitors to see reliable Covid-19 data. We also provide links that can help users determine for themselves if they or anyone they know is experiencing any symptoms of COVID-19. Similar to any other severe issues that you should report to officials as immediately as possible, you should also take responsibility to report any health risks as soon as possible. We hope you stay safe throughout this whole perocess, do your part for society, and check our site regularly in order to get a quick, reliable data for your local area, and the world.
